How to Care for Your Pig

Taking care of your pig may seem like a daunting task. However, we are here to help. Just follow these steps and you'll have one happy hog.

  • You'll need to make sure your pigs exercise regularly to avoid obesity and constipation and to stop their feet from becoming overgrown. They'll also need regular checks from a vet to make sure they're in good health.

  • Pigs need access to water at all times. They do not have the ability to perspire, so water is important to them. Some pigs drink a lot of water daily, some very little. Each pig is different regarding water intake. We use stainless steel non-tip bowls.

  • Piggies LOVE their blankets and the more they have, the more they love it! A blanket is the perfect "toy" and soft friend for your pig.
